Roast or Toast

The social opinion game where every opinion counts.

About

Roast or Toast is a casual social game where players react to funny, relatable, and unexpected everyday situations.

Decide whether each moment deserves a Roast 🔥 or a Toast ❤️, then see if you can predict what everyone else would choose.

Whether you're hanging out with friends, on a road trip, waiting in line, or just relaxing at home, every round starts a conversation.
