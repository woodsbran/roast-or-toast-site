About the game

Some moments deserve applause. Others deserve a roast.

Roast or Toast is a casual social game where you react to funny, relatable, and unexpected everyday situations. Make your choice, predict the crowd, earn Heat, and see where your opinions land.

Your privacy matters.

Roast or Toast is designed to be simple and private.

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • Game progress is stored locally on your device.
  • No personal data is sold or shared.
